Nasi Pager Khas Godong

A. Ingredients

The ingredients of the menu can be split into 4 groups; the vegetables, the seasoning, the sauce, and the sprinkle.

  1. Urap Sayuran (Vegetable Salad with Seasoned Grated Coconut):
    • Boiled papaya leaves
    • Boiled cassava leaves
    • Boiled turi flowers
    • Young mlanding leaves (Leucaena leaves)
    • Mlanding seeds (Leucaena seeds)
    • Bean sprouts
    • Basil leaves
    • Winged beans, thinly sliced
    • Long beans, cut into 4 cm pieces
  2. Seasoning Paste for Urap Sayuran:
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 4 shallots
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds
    • 2 kaffir lime leaves
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/4 of a young coconut, grated
  3. Sambal Kacang (Peanut Sauce):
    • Basic peanut sauce ingredients
  4. Uyah Goreng / Serundeng Asin (Toasted Salt):
    • Savory toasted grated coconut (a.k.a. Uyah Goreng or Serundeng Asin)

B. Instructions

  1. Prepare all the vegetables.
  2. Steam the vegetables, then remove and set aside in a bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, grind the seasoning ingredients for the urap (vegetable salad). Steam the mixture briefly to prevent it from spoiling quickly.
  4. Mix all the steamed vegetables with the seasoned coconut until evenly coated.
  5. Prepare the peanut sauce (sambal kacang). Add hot water and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. To serve Nasi Pager: Take a serving plate, add a portion of rice, top with the seasoned vegetables (urap), drizzle with the peanut sauce or pecel sauce, and finally sprinkle with uyah goreng (savory toasted coconut).
  7. Nasi Pager, a specialty from Godong-Indonesia, is ready to be served. It tastes even better when served with rempeyek (crispy peanut crackers) and bakwan sayur (vegetable fritters).
